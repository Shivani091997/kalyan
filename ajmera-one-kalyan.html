Common Questions

Frequently
asked questions

Have more questions? Our team is available 7 days a week. Call, WhatsApp, or visit our sales office at Murbad Road, Kalyan.

What is the address of Ajmera One Kalyan?
One Kalyan, Murbad Road, Shahad, Kalyan, Ulhasnagar, Maharashtra 421001. The site office is located on Murbad Road — prior appointment is compulsory for a site visit.
What is the carpet area and pricing?
1 BHK homes start from ₹42.9 Lakhs with carpet areas between 458–550 sq ft. 2 BHK homes start from ₹68.9 Lakhs with carpet areas between 550–657 sq ft. All across 22 storeys with expansive views.
How many towers are there and what is the status?
Destination One Kalyan has 4 towers in total. Towers 1, 2 and 3 are completely sold out. The final tower — our most premium offering — is now open for booking. This is the last opportunity to own a home at this landmark development.
What is the nearest railway station?
Shahad Railway Station on the Central Line is approximately 10 minutes from the project. Metro Line 5 and the upcoming Kalyan–Dombivli–Taloja Metro Line 12 will further enhance connectivity.
What amenities are available?
Over 50 amenities including infinity swimming pool, spa, jacuzzi, international club, open-air gym, golf putting greens, jogging park, digital gaming zone, mini theatre, library, senior citizen zone, kids play area, badminton court, 24x7 security and much more.
Is the project RERA registered?
Yes. MahaRERA Registration No. P51700030210. You can verify project details on the official MahaRERA website. This website is operated by ASB Group, the authorized marketing partner (MahaRERA No. A51700021052).
